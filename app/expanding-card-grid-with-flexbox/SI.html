Supplementary Data File 3
Regions displaying CN oscillations not classified as chromothripsis. These include: (i) CN oscillating profiles characterized by clusters of tandem duplications or deletions, (ii) candidate chromothripsis cases satisfying the statistical criteria but considered false positives by visual inspection, and (iii) chromosomes displaying at least 7 CN oscillations with few or no SVs mapped
Supplementary Data File 4
Large clusters of interleaved SVs (at least 20) not identified as chromothripsis by our method
